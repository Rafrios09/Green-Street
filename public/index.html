Why choose green energy?

Climate change can be mitigated, and environmental sustainability can be fostered by choosing green energy sources such as solar, wind, and hydropower. By reducing air pollution and combating global warming, green energy sources produce no or minimal greenhouse gas emissions. By diversifying energy sources and decreasing reliance on finite fossil fuel reserves, green energy contributes to the preservation of natural ecosystems as well as energy independence.
